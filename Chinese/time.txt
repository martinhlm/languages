> Time

zâoshang            early morning (sunrise - 8 am)
shàngwû             mornign
zhöngwû             noon
xiàwû               afternoon
wânshang            evenign (sunset - before midnight)

6:00 am     zâoshang liù diân

10:00 am    shànwû shí diân         上午

12:00 pm    zhöngwû shí'èr diân     中午

1:00 pm     xiàwû yï diân           下午

2:00 pm     xiàwû lîang diân        下午

8:00 pm     wânshàng bä diân        晚上


> Time sentences
    wô zâoshang liù diân qîchuáng.
        (I get up at six in the morning.)
        Wô          我        I
        qîchuàng    起床      get up


    Wô zâoshang qï diân chï zâofàn.
        (I have the breakfast at seven in the morning)
        zâofàn      早饭      breakfast
        chï         吃        eat


    Wô shàngwû jiû diân göngzuò.
        (I go to work at nine in the mornig)
        göngzuò     工作      work


    Wô zhöngwû shí'èr diân chï wûfàn
        (I have the lunch at twelve o'clock)
        wûfàn       午饭     lunch


    Wô xiàwû liù diân huí jiä
        (I go home at six in the afternoon)
        jiä         家       home


    Wô wânshang bä diân chï wânfàn
        (I have the dinner ar eight in the evening)
        wânfàn      晚饭     dinner

    Wô wânshang shíyï diân shuìjiào.
        (I go to sleep at eleven in the evening)
        shuìjiâo    睡觉    go to bed
