Food

    wûfàn       午饭      lunch
    wânfàn      晚饭      dinner


>>> TIPICAL

- Nî chï shénme?
    (What do you want to eat?)

    shénme          什么        what
    chï             吃          eat


- Wô chï jiâozi
    (I eat/have/want dumplings)

    jiâozi          饺子        dumplings
    châomiàn        炒面        fried noodles
    châofàn         炒饭        fried rice
    bäozi           包子        bun
    miantiao        面条        noodles


- Nî chï ... ma?
    (Do you eat ...?)

- Bù. wô bù chï ...
    (No, I don't want ...)



>>> FRUITS

- Nî xîhuan shénme shuîguô?
    (What fruit do you like?)

    xîhuan          喜欢        like
    shuîguô         水果        fruit


- Wô xîhuan píngguô.

    píngguô         苹果        apple
    xiangjiäo       香蕉        banana
    chéngzi         橙子        orange


- Nî wîhuan píngguô ma?
- Bù, wô bù xîhuan píngguô.



>>> WANT

- Nî yào shénme?
    (What do you want?)

    yào             要         want


- Wô yào niúròu.
    (I want beef)

    niúròu          牛肉        beef
    jïròu           鸡肉        chicken
    zhüròu          猪肉        pork
    yú              鱼          fish
    shücài          蔬菜        vegetables


- Nî yào niúròu ma?.
- Bù, wô bù yào niúròu.

- Nî xîhuan shücài ma?
    (Do you like vegetables?)

- Shì de, wô xiâng yào shücài.
    (Yes, I would like vegetables.)
