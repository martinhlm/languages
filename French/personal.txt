> PRONOUNS

    Je          I
    tu          you (singular)
    vous        you (formal)
    il          He/it
    elle        She/it
    nous        we
    vous        you (plural)
    Ils         they (any group including a male)
    elles       "elles sont rouges" - they/them (all womenx)
    êtes        "vous êtes" - (you) are
    suis        "je suis" - am (I am)
    sont        are (plural)

    garcons     boy

> SUBJECT-VERB AGREEMENT
    subject         manger(to eat)          être(to be)         avoir(to have)

    je              je mange - I eat        je suis - I am      j'ai - I have
    tu              tu manges - you eat     tu es - you are     tu as - you have
    il/elle/on      il mange - he eats      il est - he is      il a - he has


> HAVE
    avez        - "vous avez un livre" have
    avons       - "nous avons un chat"
