> BONJOUR

    Greetings: bonjour, bonsoir (plus bon matin in Québec only)
    Farewells: bonne journée, bon après-midi, bonne soirée, bonne nuit


> LIASIONS
    In a liaison, an otherwise silent ending consonant is pushed to the next word,
    where it's pronounced as part of the first syllable.


    Oui                         Yes
    Non                         No/not

    Bonjour                     Hello/Good mornign/Good afternoon
    Bonsoir                     Good evening
    Bonne nuit                  Good night
    Salut                       Hi/Hello   Bye/goodbye
    Bienvenue                   Welcome
    Au revoir                   Goodbye

    D'accord                    All right/Right

    Merci                       Thank you/Thanks
    Merci beaucop               Thank you very much

    Pardon                      Excuse me/Pardon me
    Excuse moi
    Désolé                      Sorry

    S'il te plaît               Please/If you please
    S'il vous plaît

    Comment ça va ?             How are you?/How are you doing?
    ça va                       I'm fine/How are you(?)/I am doing well
    ça va bien (?)              It is going well/Are you doing well(?)/How is it going(?)

    De rien                     You're welcome/It's nothing/Don't mention it

    À plus tard                 See you later
    À bientôt                   See you soon
    À demain                    See you tomorrow

    Il y a                      There is/there are/ago
